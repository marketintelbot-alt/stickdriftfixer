Editorial Policy

How Content Is Produced and Corrected

Policy date: February 28, 2026.

Scope

We publish only content related to controller diagnostics, stick drift behavior, calibration methods, and repair decisions.

Quality Standards

  • Use clear procedures with reproducible steps.
  • State limitations and uncertainty explicitly.
  • Avoid copy-only pages and placeholder content.
  • Update or remove outdated recommendations.

Corrections

If a material issue is identified, we update the affected section and revise the page update date. Corrections can be requested through the Contact page.
