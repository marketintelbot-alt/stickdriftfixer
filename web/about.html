About This Publisher

Why This Site Exists

Stick Drift Fixer is a focused resource for controller users who want clear troubleshooting guidance and practical calibration support.

Editorial Focus

We publish only content related to controller input diagnostics, calibration, and repair decision workflows. The main goal is to reduce guesswork and help users choose the right next step based on measured behavior.

Content is written to be task-oriented: what to test first, what each result means, and when software mitigation is no longer enough.

Methodology

  • Use repeatable test steps across multiple platforms.
  • Separate software configuration issues from hardware wear.
  • Document limitations and avoid claiming permanent software repair.
  • Review and refresh pages when platform behavior changes.

Transparency

Policy pages and contact information are publicly available in the footer on every page. If we publish a correction, the update date is changed and the corrected section is rewritten for clarity.
