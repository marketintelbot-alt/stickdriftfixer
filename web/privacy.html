How Data Is Handled

Information We Process

The browser calibration tool runs locally in your browser. Calibration profiles are stored only in your local browser storage unless you export and share them.

We do not ask for account creation to use the tool. If you contact us by email, we process the information you provide to respond.

Advertising and Cookies

This site may use Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies to personalize or measure ads where applicable. See Google's privacy and ad technology resources for details.

Third Parties

Third-party services may include hosting and advertising providers. Their data practices are governed by their own policies.

Your Choices

  • Clear local browser storage to remove saved calibration profiles.
  • Disable personalized ads through Google ad settings where supported.
  • Contact us for policy questions at the address on the Contact page.
